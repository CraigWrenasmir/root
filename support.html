ROOT — Support

How to play

Turn your device sideways. Move with the on-screen arrows, QUERY to touch what you find, /ROOT to see the files you have downloaded. You are @, the Reader. Walk to the edges to find routes the archive has not grown yet — and return after the next night to find new rooms.

The world keeps growing

New rooms appear every night. If you open the app and it looks larger than you remember, that is intended — the archive dreams new places while you are away.
